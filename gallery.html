Some photos and videos of the band in action.

The second in our St david's Day video series - here's our virtual performance of the wonderful Welsh traditional melody 'Ar Hyd y Nos'. From all of us at Briton Ferry Silver band - Happy St David's Day to all our friends and followers!

Following on from last years virtual carol concert, we decided to take what we learned from that project and create a new series of videos for Spring 2021. Enjoy the first of these - a wonderful piece of music, chosen to coincide with today's Wales V England match and St David's Day on Monday. Please enjoy our rendition of Gordon Langford's arrangement of the classic "Men Of Harlech".

2020 has been a pretty quiet year for live performances, as you might imagine. The band has been unable to get together to rehearse since the spring, but that hasn't stopped us! We've created a virtual carol concert via the wonders of modern technology, and we're excited to share it with all our friends and supporters. Grab yourselves a glass of mulled wine, light the fire, sit back and enjoy our online Christmas concert 2020! Seasons greetings all!
